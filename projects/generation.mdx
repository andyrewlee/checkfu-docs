Tell Checkfu what you need and AI creates it for you. This is the fastest way to create educational content.

## How It Works

1. Click **New Project**
2. Choose a project type (Worksheet or Slides)
3. Describe what you want
4. AI generates the content
5. Edit as needed

## Project Types

### Worksheets
Practice problems, homework, assessments. You'll get:
- Problem sets and exercises
- Word problems with illustrations
- Fill-in-the-blank activities
- Multiple choice questions

### Slides
Lesson presentations. You'll get:
- Clear visual hierarchy
- Key concepts and definitions
- Instructional diagrams

## Writing Good Prompts

The better your description, the better the result. Include:

### Grade Level
```
"3rd grade multiplication practice"
"High school chemistry review"
"Adult ESL vocabulary worksheet"
```

### Topic and Scope
```
"Introduction to fractions - identifying and comparing"
"Photosynthesis process with labeled diagrams"
"Spanish verb conjugation - present tense regular verbs"
```

### Specific Requirements
```
"10 word problems about money, increasing difficulty"
"Include answer blanks and a scoring section"
"Visual examples with step-by-step solutions"
```

## Using Sources

You can upload PDFs or documents as **Sources** to give AI more context. This helps when you want content that:
- Matches your curriculum
- Follows a specific textbook's style
- Aligns to particular standards

1. Go to **Sources** and upload your documents
2. When generating, click **Add Sources**
3. Select relevant pages
4. AI uses them as reference

## Generation Settings

### Audience
- **Grade Level**: Pre-K through Adult
- **Custom**: Describe your audience (e.g., "ESL beginners")

### Standards
Add specific standards to align content:
- Common Core standards
- State standards
- Your own learning objectives

### Page Count
Choose how many pages to generate.
