This explains what students see when they work through your courses.

## Finding Courses

Students log in and see their dashboard with:
- Courses they're enrolled in
- Progress for each
- What to work on next

They click a course to enter it.

## Inside a Course

Students see:
- Course name and description
- List of sections and lessons
- Progress bar showing overall completion
- Status icons for each lesson

### Sequential vs. Flexible

**Sequential courses:**
- Lessons unlock in order
- Must finish one to access the next
- Locked lessons show a lock icon

**Flexible courses:**
- All lessons available from the start
- Students choose what order
- Can revisit any lesson

## Working Through a Lesson

A typical lesson has:

1. **Introduction** - Text or video explaining what they'll learn
2. **Projects** - Worksheets or materials to complete
3. **Quiz** (optional) - Questions to test understanding

### Completing Projects

When students open a worksheet:

1. Read instructions
2. Navigate between pages using arrows
3. Fill in answers in interactive fields
4. Click the chat icon for help from AI tutor
5. Click **Submit** when done

### Saving Progress

- Work auto-saves as they go
- They can leave and come back
- "In progress" shows incomplete work
- "Submitted" shows finished work

## Taking Quizzes

### Starting

1. Complete required projects (if any)
2. Click **Start Quiz**
3. Answer one question at a time

### During the Quiz

- Questions adapt to performance
- Can skip and come back
- Can flag questions for review
- Progress bar shows how far along

### Quiz Endings

The quiz ends when:
- They demonstrate mastery
- They reach the target score
- They hit max questions
- They choose to end early

### Results

After finishing:
- Overall score
- Correct vs. incorrect
- Areas that need work
- Option to retake (if allowed)
