This guide shows you where everything is. You can be up and running in a few minutes.

## The Sidebar

The left sidebar is how you get around. Here's what each section does:

| Section | What's There |
|---------|--------------|
| **Projects** | Your worksheets and slides |
| **Sources** | PDFs and documents you've uploaded for AI to reference |
| **Courses** | Lessons organized for students to work through |
| **Students** | Who's enrolled and how they're doing |
| **Agents** | Your AI tutors that help students |
| **Settings** | Team members |

## Your Projects

Click **Projects** to see all your worksheets and materials.

Each project shows:
- A preview image
- The name
- When it was last edited

**Click any project to open it** in the editor where you can view, edit, print, or share it.

## The Editor

When you open a project, you'll see three areas:

### Left: Pages
All the pages in your project. Click to switch between them.

### Center: Canvas
The main area where you view and edit content.
- **Zoom**: Ctrl/Cmd + scroll
- **Pan**: Hold Space and drag

### Right: Inspector
When you select something, this panel shows its settings.

## Printing

To print or download a project:

1. Open the project
2. Click the **menu** (three dots) in the left panel
3. Choose **Print** or **Export PDF**

<Tip>
For best results, set your browser to "Letter" size and margins to "None."
</Tip>

## Your Courses

Click **Courses** to see your organized learning content.

Each course shows:
- Name and status (Draft or Published)
- Number of lessons
- How many students are enrolled

**Students can only see Published courses.** Keep courses in Draft while you're building them.

## Your Students

Click **Students** to see:
- Everyone enrolled in your workspace
- Their progress through courses
- Work waiting for your review

Click on a student's name to see their detailed progress.
