The Students section shows everyone in your workspace and their progress through courses.

## The Students Page

Click **Students** in the sidebar to see:
- All students in your workspace
- Their recent activity
- Overall progress across courses

## What Students Can Do

Students with the **Student** role can:
- View published courses
- Complete lessons and quizzes
- Submit work for grading
- Chat with the AI tutor

Students **cannot**:
- Create or edit projects
- See draft courses
- View other students' work
- Change workspace settings

## Viewing a Student

Click on a student's name to see their details:

### Progress
- Courses they're enrolled in
- Completion percentage for each
- Total lessons completed

### Activity
- Lessons started and completed
- Quiz scores
- Work submitted

### Submitted Work
- Everything they've turned in
- Whether it's been graded
- Scores and feedback

## Spotting Students Who Need Help

The Students page highlights students who may be struggling:

| Sign | What It Means |
|------|---------------|
| **No recent activity** | Haven't logged in lately |
| **Struggling** | Low quiz scores or repeated failures |
| **Stuck** | Taking too long on one lesson |
| **Behind pace** | Not keeping up with expected progress |
