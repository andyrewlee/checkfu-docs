Checkfu tracks student progress at multiple levels so you can see the big picture and drill into details.

## Course Progress

Go to a course and click **Progress** to see:

| What You See | What It Means |
|--------------|---------------|
| **Enrollment** | How many students are in the course |
| **Completion rate** | Percentage who finished |
| **Average score** | Mean quiz score |
| **Time to complete** | How long students typically take |

### Student List

Each student shows:
- Lessons completed out of total
- Which lesson they're on
- Quiz average
- Last activity date

## Lesson Progress

For each lesson, you can see:

### Completion
- Not started
- In progress
- Completed

### Submissions
- Pending review (you haven't graded yet)
- Graded
- Needs revision

### Quiz Performance
- How many attempts
- Best score
- Whether they achieved mastery

## Individual Student View

Click any student to see their full picture:

### Timeline
A list of everything they've done:
- Lessons started and finished
- Work submitted
- Quizzes taken
- AI tutor conversations

### Performance Summary
- Overall progress percentage
- Quiz score trends
- Concepts mastered vs. struggling

### All Submissions
Everything they've turned in with:
- Date submitted
- Grade (if graded)
- Your feedback

   - Student names
   - Lessons completed
