## What's in a Workspace

| Section | What's There |
|---------|--------------|
| **Projects** | Your worksheets and slides |
| **Sources** | Uploaded PDFs and reference materials |
| **Courses** | Organized lessons for students |
| **Students** | Enrolled learners and their progress |
| **Agents** | Your AI tutors |
| **Settings** | Team members |

## Personal vs. Team Workspaces

### Personal Workspace
Created automatically when you sign up:
- For individual use
- Your personal projects
- Can upgrade to team workspace later

### Team Workspace
For groups working together:
- Multiple staff members
- Shared projects and courses
- Student enrollment
- Collaborative editing

## Creating a Workspace

1. Click your profile in the top-right
2. Select **Create Workspace**
3. Give it a name (e.g., "5th Grade Math")
4. Invite team members

## Switching Workspaces

If you belong to multiple workspaces:
1. Click your profile or workspace name
2. Pick from the list
3. You'll see that workspace's content
